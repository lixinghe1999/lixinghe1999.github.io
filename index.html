I am Lixing He, a PhD student of the Information Engineering Department of The Chinese University of Hong Kong as a member of AIoT Lab. Before that, I obtained Bachelor degree of Engineering at UESTC in 2021 and visited UC Berkeley in 2020. I was a research assistant at CUHK in 2021.

During my PhD journey, I was research intern at Microsoft Research Asia in 2023.

My research interests:

  1. Tiny and Mobile deep learning.
  2. sensing on wearable (especially for earable).
  3. multi-modal learning on novel applications.
